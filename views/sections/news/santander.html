HELO’s In Someone Else’s Shoes shortlisted for a Glass Lion

July 03, 2021

Bozoma Saint John, Global Chief Marketing Officer for Netflix announced the Shortlist for the Cannes Glass Lion Award. HELO’s In Someone Else’s Shoes was nominated along with other stunning work that we are truly inspired by. The Glass Lion for Change celebrates culture-shifting creativity.
The Glass Lion recognises work that implicitly or explicitly addresses issues of gender inequality or prejudice, through the conscious representation of gender in advertising. Entries need to demonstrate ideas intended to change the world; work which sets out to positively impact ingrained gender inequality, imbalance or injustice.
