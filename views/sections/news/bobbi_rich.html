Mama Hot Dog rocks out with Helo.

June 23, 2021

Bobbi “mama hot dog” Rich, the multi-faceted director/production designer/storyteller/ photographer will be joining forces with HELO to create a range of jaw dropping content. A veteran music director for artists such as Brittany Howard, Enrique Iglesias, Elton John, Nathaniel Rateliff, Marg Price and The Pixies, Bobbi transforms space into bold, visceral experiences.
Rich started her career as a story and field producer for TV series that included American Pickers and Going Cambo. She was the art director for the Joe Berlinger Netflix series Crime Scene, and the production designer for the Vimeo Staff pick, Assholes. She spent 2020 on the creative direction & interior design of Nashville’s new experiential ‘70s inspired hotspot, The Dive Motel & Swim Club.
She is currently working on several projects, including a documentary about music industry legend Phil Kaufman, titled The Road Mangler, a documentary series titled Mother Truckers Convoy, and the series titled Swipe Left.
Bobbi particularly thrives as a creative director working on multi-faceted projects. She’s worked with musicians, brands, networks, agencies, hotels and startups. She’s passionate about building worlds that tell stylized stories. “Bobbi is exactly the type of emerging, diverse, and fearless creator that HELO looks for”, says HELO co-founder Brendan Kiernam, “and we’re absolutely thrilled to represent her.”
