Biography

Born a military brat and finally settling in Rockville, Maryland - Jeff Tremaine gravitated towards the alternative world of skateboarding, BMX, reggae music, and art, pursuits that would shape a career to come. After attending Washington University, Tremaine headed West, where he worked at Go magazine, then as art/editorial director for Big Brother — where he, Spike Jonze, and Johnny Knoxville laid the seeds for Jackass, a television series that would become a cultural phenomenon. The franchise now includes Jackass: The Movie, Jackass Number Two, Jackass 2.5, and Jackass 3D. Tremaine’s feature, Bad Grandpa, has grossed over $150M worldwide and his newest addition Jackass Forever opens in February 2022 . As a commercial director, Tremaine brings his unique vision to comedy, celebrity, and experiential campaigns, creating iconic and award-winning work for clients such as; Bud Light, Allstate, Comcast, Burger King, and American Airlines.
