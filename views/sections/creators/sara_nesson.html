Biography

Sara is an OSCAR and EMMY nominated and International Documentary Association (IDA) winning documentary film director, best known for her films “Poster Girl” and “Women Who Score.” She is also a vérité style cinematographer, shooting much of her own work which allows for deeper intimacy with her subjects. With her visceral and artistic sensibility, Sara thrives on telling stories that spark emotional connections and fuels our joy of discovery.

She has helmed a range of commercial projects and created content for iconic names such as Cirque de Soleil, Bonaroo Music Festival, and ICM Partners.

Mentored by her adventure-seeking, documentary filmmaker dad, a filming expedition left them stranded on a Siberian peninsula after a boat went awry. That life altering experience along with working as a sea kayaking guide in Alaska taught her a thing or two about powering through any challenge. She can still pull off an Eskimo Roll.
