Biography

Shifting paths from a photography degree to film at the University of Central Florida has led Mike on an eclectic path in storytelling. His work has been featured in an assortment of venues, from airline seat screens and in-app ads to feature films on cable networks, prime time playoff game commercials, screen projections for large scale events and narrative video games. His recent client list reflects the diversity in his work from household names like Burger King, Puma, United Airlines, BP and Chick-fil-A to beauty brands Fresh and Deva Curl to punk rock legends Epitaph Records, there is a good chance you have seen his work in the wild. He remains passionate about still photography and the ways in which technology and storytelling are melding.
