Biography

Born out of genuine passion and excitement for creating theatre, Les Enfants Terribles was founded by Artistic Director Oliver Lansley, with longtime collaborator James Seager coming on board as an associate producer in 2005. The company has since grown from small 50-seat venues to large-scale events worldwide. Les Enfants Terribles has continued to receive critical acclaim and has been nominated for and won top industry awards, establishing a large and loyal following. The company has toured extensively all over the UK and performed productions to thousands in theatres in Poland, Czech Republic, Dubai, Norway, Singapore and Australia. Their Les Enfants Experiences, create innovative experiences for brand partners, such as creative installations for Edward Sharpe and The Magnetic Zeros, to events for The Lyric Hammersmith, Latitude Festivals, HP Computers, Disney, Petersham Playhouse, the V&A Museum, The Mint Hotels, Heineken and many others.
