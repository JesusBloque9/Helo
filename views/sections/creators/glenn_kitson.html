Biography

Glenn Kitson is a UK-born director and original founder of magazine & creative studio The Rig Out.

Recognised as a London based tastemaker working with and directing for some of the biggest and best names in music, lifestyle, fashion and performance sports, Glenn started out as a stylist before working as a creative director at RSA Films (Ridley Scott Associates) which led him to jump behind the lens as a director in his own right.

Today, he is hailed for his honest voice, witty scriptwriting and innovative thinking, working with established artists, subcultures and influencers to create films that capture the minds of youth, and generates an audience on a global scale.

Kitson’s client list spans Ralph Lauren, Nike, Levi’s, Reebok, Converse, Adidas, Tate Modern and more. Recent work includes Glenn’s spot for AKQA and Nike for their 2018 World Cup starring England soccer captain, Harry Kane, music videos for quirky London upstart, Jimothy Lacoste and campaigns for Polo Ralph Lauren.
