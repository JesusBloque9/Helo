Biography

Kyle Ruddick is a film director and experience designer. He comes from a career in VFX, animation, and post-production, most notably with his work with LucasFilm on the Star Wars Franchise. He is the founder of documentary film project, One Day on Earth, which produced the first film made in every country of the world, created by thousands of volunteer filmmakers, non-profits, and The United Nations. He followed up this global effort with a PBS series on the future of the American city. In the last 2 years, he has completed work for Minute Maid, The Player’s Tribune, The Ad Council, Fan Duel, Cheerios, United Airlines, Fan Duel, Dick’s Sporting Goods, Jeane-O, and Cascadian Farm. He is also the creator of the “Magic Dance Mirror”, an augmented reality dance experience seen everywhere from E3, Burning Man, Future of Storytelling, and Slamdance. His next ventures include multiple film projects, the creation of a social video platform called “Hashvids”, and expanding his augmented reality company, Kinetic Magic.
