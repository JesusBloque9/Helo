Sebastian Kite Bringing art to commerce through extraordinary installations.

Biography

Sebastian Kite is an installation artist, architect and musician who creates immersive installations and environmental design. He holds a BA in Architecture at the Glasgow School of Art and an MA in Film, Time and Architecture at Westminster School of Architecture. His expertise and artistry work with materials; light, kinetics, sound, structure, moving-image, technology and performance.

He was driven to create a new way of experiencing brands through the symbiotic relationship between artist practice and the commercial realm. Kite’s creative approach is to perceive the brand as time, material and space. He draws the essence, the ephemeral understanding of what the brand truly means, to create immersive experiences.

Kite works with a diverse sector such as aviation, innovative technology, luxury, fashion and retail. His clientele includes Adidas, Coca Cola, American Airlines, Y-3, Hermès, Lange & Söhne, Nivea and Royal Salute.
